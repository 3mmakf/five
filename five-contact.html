05 — FAQ

When should we start working with you?+
If you're launching a new brand, start talking to us 6–8 months before you plan to go to market. Typical end-to-end identity projects take a minimum of 12 weeks, and the full system can take 6 months from first conversation to launch. The earlier, the better — because the beginning is sacred.
What does "five senses" actually mean for branding?+
We believe your customer feels your brand before they understand it. The weight of your packaging, the cadence of your copy, the color that stops them mid-scroll — these aren't just aesthetic choices. They're conversion mechanisms. We map every service to one of the five senses as a reminder that branding is a full-body experience, not just a visual exercise.
Do you work with brands that are just starting out?+
That's our focus. We're built for founders in beauty, fitness, and fashion who are making decisions with incomplete information, limited runway, and everything on the line — and who still need to show up looking like they belong at the table. We work within your constraints and prioritize ruthlessly.
How big is your team?+
Five people. One specialist for each sense and corresponding service discipline. There's no B team here. When you work with FIVE, you work with the five of us. That's the model.
What industries do you focus on?+
Our primary verticals are beauty, fitness, and fashion — industries where sensation is not supplemental to the product, it is the product. We also work in adjacent spaces like wellness, food & beverage, and lifestyle. If your brand is about how something feels, we're probably the right fit.
Do you do project or retainer work?+
Most engagements start as projects. From there, some evolve into ongoing retainers — particularly for brands who want a creative partner through their growth phase. In your first two years, the identity will need to flex with you, and we're built for that.
